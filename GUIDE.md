# PlayerShop

Turn any chest into a player-run shop. No commands needed — set it up with a shovel and sell anything you want.

---

## Creating a Shop

1. Hold any **shovel**
2. **Shift+right-click** a chest
3. The chest opens — put in the items you want to sell, then close it
4. A **price GUI** opens automatically — set your price per stack
5. Hit **Confirm** — your shop is live

> All items in the chest must be the **same type**. Mixed stacks cancel the setup.

---

## Setting the Price

The price GUI uses colored buttons to adjust the amount:

| Buttons | Change |
|---|---|
| Green +0.10 / +1 / +10 / +100 | Increase price |
| Red −0.10 / −1 / −10 / −100 | Decrease price |
| Reset | Back to $0.00 |
| Confirm | Save and go live |

Price is **per full stack (64 items)**. Buyers who buy fewer items pay proportionally.

> Example: price set to $8.00 → buying 32 items costs $4.00

---

## Restocking

Just **right-click your own chest** normally — it opens for you so you can add more items.

---

## Buying from a Shop

1. **Right-click** a shop chest
2. Use the **+/− buttons** to choose how many you want
3. Hit **Confirm** — items go to your inventory, money goes to the owner

The GUI shows you the per-item price, total cost, and current stock before you confirm.

---

## The Hologram

Every shop has a floating label above the chest showing:

- Owner name
- Item being sold
- Price per stack (and per-item breakdown)
- Current stock count
- "Right-click to buy"

Unconfigured shops display: *Shop / Place items to sell*

---

## Tips

- You receive payment **even if you're offline**
- **Breaking your shop chest deletes the shop** — be careful
- If you want to sell a different item, redo setup: shift+shovel the chest, empty it, add the new item, close
- Free shops ($0.00) are blocked by default
- Double chests work as shops too
